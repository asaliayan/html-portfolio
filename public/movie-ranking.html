The best movies According to me

My top 3 movies for all the time

John Wick: Chapter 4

John Wick uncovers a path to defeating The High Table. But before he can earn his freedom, Wick must face off against a new enemy with powerful alliances across the globe and forces that turn old friends into foes

Oppenheimer

his biographical drama tells the story of American scientist J. Robert Oppenheimer and his role in the development of the atomic bomb. Directed by Christopher Nolan, the movie stars Cillian Murphy, Emily Blunt, Matt Damon, and Robert Downey Jr.

A Thousand and One

This movie is a drama that tells the story of a young woman who is forced to confront her past when she returns to her hometown. Directed by Clarisa Navas, the movie stars Sofia Gala Castiglione, Lautaro Bettoni, and Ana Carolina Garcia
